Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Exchange Rate Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Exchange Rate Tracker does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app. The app is designed to work immediately without any sign-up or registration.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our Service, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Information: Features you use, screens you view, and time spent in the app
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, app crashes, and system activity

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our Service
  • Fetch real-time exchange rate data from trusted sources
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and bugs
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our Service

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 No Personal Data Sharing

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not share, sell, or rent your personal data to third parties.

3.2 Service Providers

We use third-party service providers to help us operate our Service, including:

  • Firebase (Google): Cloud Functions for fetching exchange rate data
  • Exchange Data Providers: Third-party APIs for real-time exchange rates

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Storage and Security

We use Firebase services provided by Google to fetch exchange rate data. No user data is stored on our servers. All exchange rate data is fetched in real-time and displayed to you without being stored persistently.

While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

Since we do not collect or store personal information, there is no user data to retain or delete. Exchange rate data is fetched in real-time and not stored on your device or our servers.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 No Account Data

Since the app does not require an account and does not collect personal information, there is no account data to access, update, or delete.

6.2 App Deletion

You can stop all data collection by simply uninstalling the app from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. The app is suitable for all ages as it only displays exchange rate information.

8. International Data Transfers

Exchange rate data may be fetched from servers located in different countries. We use Firebase services which comply with applicable data protection regulations including GDPR.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: derushev.aleksei@gmail.com
  • Support: Exchange Rate Tracker Support

11. Third-Party Services

11.1 Firebase Services

We use Firebase Cloud Functions provided by Google LLC to fetch exchange rate data. Firebase's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy, available at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

11.2 Exchange Data Providers

We fetch exchange rate data from third-party cryptocurrency exchanges and data providers. We do not control their privacy practices and recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies.

12. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have certain rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). However, since we do not collect personal information, these rights do not apply to our Service.

13. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since we do not collect personal data, these rights do not apply to our Service.

The app processes exchange rate data solely for the purpose of displaying it to you. No personal data is collected, stored, or processed.
