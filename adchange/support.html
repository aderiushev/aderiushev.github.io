Support

Welcome to Exchange Rate Tracker support. Find answers to common questions below, or contact us directly if you need additional assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the app?

Simply download and open Exchange Rate Tracker. The app will immediately display live exchange rates for RUB→USDT, USDT→EUR, and RUB→EUR. No account or sign-up required. Swipe between tabs to view different currency pairs.

Do I need to create an account?

No! Exchange Rate Tracker works without any account creation or sign-up. Just download the app and start viewing exchange rates immediately.

Is the app free?

Yes, Exchange Rate Tracker is completely free to download and use. There are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or hidden fees.

What currency pairs are available?

The app currently tracks three currency pairs:

  • RUB → USDT: Russian Ruble to Tether (USDT)
  • USDT → EUR: Tether (USDT) to Euro
  • RUB → EUR: Russian Ruble to Euro (both direct and indirect via USDT)

How often are rates updated?

Exchange rates are fetched in real-time from trusted cryptocurrency exchanges. Pull down on any screen to refresh and get the latest rates. The app also automatically refreshes rates when you return to it from the background.

What is the difference between direct and indirect RUB→EUR rates?

The RUB→EUR screen shows two rates:

  • Direct: The direct exchange rate from RUB to EUR
  • Indirect: The rate calculated by converting RUB→USDT→EUR (going through USDT)

Comparing these rates can help you find the best conversion path for your needs.

Where does the exchange rate data come from?

Exchange rate data is fetched from reliable cryptocurrency exchanges and data providers. The app displays the exchange name and source for each rate, ensuring transparency about where the data comes from.

Can I use the app offline?

No, Exchange Rate Tracker requires an internet connection to fetch real-time exchange rates. Without connectivity, the app cannot display current rates.

How do I refresh the rates?

Pull down on any screen to refresh and get the latest exchange rates. The app uses the standard pull-to-refresh gesture found in most mobile apps.

What platforms are supported?

Exchange Rate Tracker is currently available for iOS devices. The app is built with React Native, which allows for potential Android support in the future.

Why are the rates different from other sources?

Exchange rates can vary between different exchanges and data providers. The rates shown in our app are fetched from specific cryptocurrency exchanges and may differ from rates offered by banks, other exchanges, or currency converters. Always verify rates before making any financial transactions.

Can I use these rates for actual currency exchange?

The rates displayed in Exchange Rate Tracker are for informational purposes only. They should be used as a reference, not as the sole basis for financial transactions. Always verify current rates with your exchange or financial institution before making any trades or conversions.

The app won't load rates. What should I do?

If rates aren't loading, try these steps:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Pull down to refresh the screen
  • Close and reopen the app
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you have the latest version of the app

If the problem persists, please contact us at derushev.aleksei@gmail.com.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Exchange Rate Tracker does not collect or store any personal information. The app simply fetches exchange rate data and displays it to you. No account, no personal data, no tracking. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

We'd love to hear from you! Please email us at derushev.aleksei@gmail.com with details about the bug or your feature request. We review all feedback and use it to improve the app.

Can I add more currency pairs?

Currently, the app supports RUB, USDT, and EUR. If you'd like to see additional currency pairs, please let us know by emailing derushev.aleksei@gmail.com. We're always considering new features based on user feedback.
