Support

Get help with Control Manager

Welcome to Control Manager support. Find answers to common questions below, or contact us directly if you need additional assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download the Control Manager app from the App Store, tap "Sign Up", and enter your email address and password. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account. Click the link in the email to verify and start using the app.

How do I invite team members?

Open the app, navigate to Settings, and tap "Team Members". Tap the "Invite" button, enter the email address of the person you want to invite, and send the invitation. They'll receive an email with a link to join your team.

How do I create a task?

From the "My Tasks" screen, tap the "New Task" button at the bottom. Enter the task details, including title, description, and due date. You can also assign the task to a team member if needed. Tap "Save" to create the task.

How do I assign a task to someone?

When creating or editing a task, tap the "Assignee" field and select a team member from the list. The task will appear in their "Assigned to Me" view, and they'll receive a notification.

What are the different task views?

Control Manager offers three main views:

  • My Tasks: Tasks you created that haven't been assigned yet
  • All Tasks: All tasks in your team, including those created by others
  • Assigned to Me: Tasks that have been assigned to you by team members

How do I track time on tasks?

Open a task and tap the "Worklog" button. You can add time entries with descriptions to track how much time you've spent on the task. This helps monitor productivity and understand time allocation.

How do I organize tasks into projects?

When creating or editing a task, tap the "Project" field and either select an existing project or create a new one. Projects help you group related tasks together for better organization.

Can I use Control Manager offline?

Control Manager requires an internet connection to sync data in real-time with your team. While you can view previously loaded tasks offline, creating or updating tasks requires connectivity.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, please contact us at derushev.aleksei@gmail.com with your account email address. We'll process your request and delete your account and associated data.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Control Manager uses Firebase services provided by Google, which includes industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest. We take security seriously and implement best practices to protect your information. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive an email with a link to reset your password. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password.

Can I use Control Manager on multiple devices?

Yes! Your account and all your tasks sync automatically across all devices where you're logged in. Changes made on one device appear instantly on all other devices.

What platforms does Control Manager support?

Control Manager is currently available for iOS devices. We're working on bringing the app to additional platforms in the future.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

We'd love to hear from you! Please email us at derushev.aleksei@gmail.com with details about the bug or your feature request. We review all feedback and use it to improve the app.
