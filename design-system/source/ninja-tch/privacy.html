Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ninja-tch ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

ninja-tch does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app. The app is designed to work immediately without any sign-up or registration.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

ninja-tch does not automatically collect any information. The app operates entirely via local Bluetooth Low Energy connections between devices. No data is sent to any server.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since ninja-tch operates entirely offline via Bluetooth Low Energy, we do not collect, store, or process any user data. All messages are exchanged directly between devices and are not stored persistently.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 No Data Collection

Since we do not collect any information, we do not share, sell, or rent any data to third parties.

3.2 Local Communication Only

All messages are transmitted directly between two devices via Bluetooth Low Energy. No data passes through any server, cloud service, or third party.

4. Data Storage and Security

ninja-tch does not store messages or any user data on external servers. All communication happens locally over BLE. Messages exist only during the active session and are not persisted after the app is closed.

5. Data Retention

Since we do not collect or store any data, there is no data to retain or delete. Messages exchanged over BLE are transient and exist only during the active session.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 No Account Data

Since the app does not require an account and does not collect any information, there is no data to access, update, or delete.

6.2 App Deletion

You can uninstall the app at any time. Since no data is collected or stored, uninstalling the app completely removes all traces of the application.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. The app is suitable for all ages as it only facilitates local BLE messaging between devices.

8. Bluetooth Permissions

ninja-tch requires Bluetooth permissions to function. These permissions are used solely for discovering nearby devices and establishing BLE connections for messaging. No location data or Bluetooth data is collected, stored, or transmitted to any server.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: derushev.aleksei@gmail.com
  • Support: ninja-tch Support

11. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have certain rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). However, since we do not collect personal information, these rights do not apply to our Service.

12. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since we do not collect personal data, these rights do not apply to our Service.

The app does not collect, store, or process any personal data. All communication is local and peer-to-peer.
