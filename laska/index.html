Features

Everything you need to show up for each other.

A warm, thoughtful toolkit that turns effort into play — and play into closeness.

📋

Task system

Create meaningful tasks for your partner across six categories — Home, Romance, Talk, Intimacy, Self-care and Custom — with an approval flow that builds communication.

✨

Love Points economy

Earn LP from completed tasks, auto-split between your personal balance and the couple bank. Level up together through six warm tiers as your bond grows.

🗺️

Journeys

Guided multi-day programs from 3 to 30 days. Unlock a step a day and earn bonus LP for finishing a journey side by side.

🎁

Rewards

Redeem points for 150+ template coupons or design your own. Negotiate prices with your partner and gift each other experiences that matter.

🎯

Shared goals

Pool LP in your couple bank toward big milestones. Smart forecasts show how many weeks until you reach each goal at your current pace.

💬

Daily questions

Three conversation starters surface each day from a curated set of 5,000+. Pick one together, earn bonus LP — no repeats within 90 days.
