Features

Everything you need to nurture your relationship through play and shared effort.

📋

Task System

Create meaningful tasks for your partner and earn Love Points. Six categories — Home, Romance, Talk, Intimacy, Self-care, and Custom — with an approval workflow that builds communication.

✨

Love Points Economy

Earn LP from completed tasks, auto-split between personal balance and couple bank. Level up together through 6 tiers as your relationship grows stronger.

🗺️

Journeys

Guided multi-day relationship programs spanning 3 to 30 days. Unlock steps sequentially and earn bonus LP for completing each journey together.

🎁

Rewards

Redeem Love Points for 150+ template coupons or create custom rewards. Negotiate prices with your partner and gift each other meaningful experiences.

🎯

Shared Goals

Save LP together in your Couple Bank toward big milestones. Smart forecasts show how many weeks until you reach each goal at your current pace.

💬

Daily Questions

Three conversation starters surface daily from a curated database of 5,000+ questions. Pick one together and earn bonus LP — no repeats within 90 days.
