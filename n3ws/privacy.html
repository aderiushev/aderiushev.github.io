Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how N3ws ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 News Content

The App retrieves news headlines from third-party sources via the Perplexity AI API. This content is cached locally on your device for performance optimization.

1.2 Local Storage

We store the following data locally on your device using AsyncStorage:

  • Cached news headlines (refreshed every 5 minutes)
  • Timestamp of last news fetch
  • App preferences and settings

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and usage statistics
  • Error logs and crash reports (to improve app stability)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Core Functionality: To fetch, cache, and display news headlines
  • Improve Performance: To optimize caching and reduce API calls
  • Improve the App: To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and enhance user experience
  • Communication: To respond to your support requests and provide customer service

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using React Native's AsyncStorage. We do not maintain any servers or databases that store your personal information.

News content is cached temporarily and automatically refreshed every 5 minutes. You can clear this cache at any time by reinstalling the app.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

4.1 Perplexity AI

We use Perplexity AI's API to fetch and generate 3-word news headlines. Perplexity may collect and process data according to their Privacy Policy. Learn more at: https://www.perplexity.ai/privacy

When the App requests news, the following information may be sent to Perplexity:

  • API request parameters (news category, language)
  • Timestamp of the request

No personally identifiable information is sent to Perplexity.

4.2 News Sources

The App may link to external news sources. When you tap on a headline to read the full article, you will be directed to the original source website. These websites have their own privacy policies and are not controlled by N3ws.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With Perplexity AI to provide news content
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety

6. No User Accounts

N3ws does not require user accounts or authentication. You can use the App anonymously without providing any personal information such as email, name, or phone number.

7. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, we do not retain any data on our servers. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the App.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: All data is stored locally on your device and accessible only to you
  • Deletion: Uninstall the App to delete all locally stored data
  • Control: You control when to refresh news and how often you use the App

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

The Perplexity AI API may process data in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that are different from the laws of your country. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
