Frequently Asked Questions

What is N3ws?

N3ws is a minimalist news app that delivers the latest headlines in exactly 3 words. We use AI to distill news stories from Russia, Spain, and around the world into ultra-concise summaries, helping you stay informed without information overload.

How often does the news update?

News is refreshed every 5 minutes. The app uses smart caching to minimize data usage and API calls. When you open the app, you'll see a countdown timer showing when fresh news will be available. You can tap the "+3" button to manually refresh when new content is ready.

What languages are supported?

N3ws provides news in three languages:

  • Russian - News from Russia in Russian
  • Spanish - News from Spain in Spanish
  • English - World news in English

Why only 3 words?

Three words is the perfect balance between brevity and meaning. It forces the AI to capture the absolute essence of each story, cutting through clickbait and fluff. You can scan dozens of headlines in seconds, then tap any that interest you to read the full article from the source.

How do I read the full article?

Tap on any 3-word headline to open the full article from the original news source in your browser. Each headline links directly to the source article, so you can dive deeper into stories that catch your attention.

How do I refresh the news?

Simply tap the button at the bottom of the screen. If fresh news is available, you'll see a "+3" button. If the cache is still fresh, you'll see a countdown timer showing when new content will be ready. The app prevents unnecessary refreshes to save data and API costs.

Does N3ws require an account?

No! N3ws is completely anonymous. You don't need to create an account, provide an email, or sign in. Just download the app and start reading news immediately.

Is N3ws free?

Yes, N3ws is completely free to download and use. There are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or ads.

How accurate are the 3-word headlines?

The headlines are generated by Perplexity AI, which analyzes current news stories and creates intelligent summaries. While we strive for accuracy, AI-generated summaries may not capture every nuance of a story. Always tap through to read the full article for complete context.

What data does N3ws collect?

Very little! N3ws stores news headlines locally on your device for caching purposes. We don't collect personal information, track your reading habits, or require accounts. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Which platforms is N3ws available on?

N3ws is currently available for iOS. Android support may be added in the future.

The app isn't loading news. What should I do?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Force close the app and reopen it
  • Wait for the cache timer to expire and try refreshing again
  • Restart your device
  • Reinstall the app (this will clear the cache)

If the problem persists, please contact us at the email below.

Can I customize which categories I see?

Currently, N3ws shows all three categories (Russia, Spain, World) by default. Customization features may be added in future updates based on user feedback.
