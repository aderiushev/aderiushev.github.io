Tot el camp

One tap. The Barça match. On your screen.

A native macOS app that finds today's live FC Barcelona match on gooool365.com, extracts the acestream:// URL, and hands it off to your favourite acestream player. Bundled Tor fallback punches through ISP blocks — no VPN, no setup, no terminal.

What it does

A focused tool for one specific job: get the Barça match playing on macOS, fast.

👆

One-tap watch flow

Open the app, tap Watch. The app fetches today's schedule, finds the live Barça match, extracts the stream URL — all inside one button press.

🧅

Tor fallback, bundled

Your ISP blocks gooool365? The app ships its own tor binary and falls back automatically. No VPN, no Tor Browser install, no DNS hacks.

🔗

Plays nice with your player

Hands off the acestream:// URL to whichever app you've registered as the handler — Televisor recommended. No bundled player, no Electron.

🦴

Native, no junk

Pure SwiftUI + AppKit. No Electron, no Chromium, no Node runtime. Tiny binary, fast launch, plays well with the rest of macOS.

📦

Self-contained DMG

Drag TotElCamp.app into Applications and you're done. Tor binary, dependencies, fonts — all bundled inside the .app. Zero prerequisites.

🛠

Open source, personal use

Built for personal use, source on GitHub. Built with XcodeGen, dylibbundler, and a single build-app.sh that produces the DMG.

How it works

Four steps. One button on the user's side.

01

Tap Watch

You open Tot el camp and press the big Watch button. That's the entire user surface.

02

Fetch the schedule

The app pulls today's matches from gooool365.com — direct first, then Tor fallback if your ISP blocks the domain.

03

Extract the stream

It locates the live Barça game and pulls the acestream:// URL out of the match page.

04

Hand off to your player

macOS opens the URL with whichever app handles acestream://. Televisor (free) is the easy choice.
